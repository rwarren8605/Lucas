**Card** — the base content surface: 20px radius, soft low-contrast shadow, white (`default`) or gray (`recessed`) background. `brand` gives the yellow hero-banner look; `dark` for the celebration footer.

```jsx
<Card>Module content…</Card>
<Card tone="brand" padding={20}>Finish all modules to unlock closing…</Card>
```

Keep corners soft — never square a card.
